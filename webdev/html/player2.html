Jenia Grebennikov

Notable Achievements

1999
— discovered volleyball at the age of 9 and joined Rennes EC, his father's club the following year
2008
— professionally debuted with Rennes Volley 35 at 18.
2010
— elected best libero in the French Championship
2011
— won the Coupe de France with his club and joined the national team.
2013
— played for German club Friedrichshafen, was finalist of German Championship, best libero of the world cup the next year.
2015
— recruited by of the best clubs in the world, Lube based in Italy.
2018
— played in Italian Clubs Trentino and Modena for the next 3 years.

Why do you like this player?

Jenia Grebennikov has caught my eye with his amazing defensive skills. With accurate positioning and quick reaction time, he will deny points for even the strongest of spikers. Growing up, Jenia was not the biggest among his peers and had to give up the spiker position which he liked alot as a child but he dealt with the cards he was dealt and worked hard to become good at this defensive position.

This webpage was made to introduce players to Volleyball and share my love for it.
