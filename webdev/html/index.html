Introduction

Hey! I'm Matthew.

I began my journey in volleyball back in the beginning of 2019. I played as a outside hitter for my secondary school. Over the past few years my fondness of volleyball have only grown. Thus, I have decided to dedicate a platform so that volleyball players young and old alike will be interact with each other and grow together as players.

Why do you like volleyball?

I like volleyball as it is fast paced and it requires you to make good decisions so as to score points.
