Yuki Ishikawa

Notable Achievements

2005
— started playing at 9 years old, played at a national tournament with just 4 months of volleyball experience
2011
— proceeded to Seijo high school, a prestigious volleyball school, which managed to win 99 times consecutively until his graduation in 2014
2014
— moved on to Chuo university and got into the national team
2016
— debuted in Modena in the italian pro league

Why do you like this player?

Yuki Ishikawa is a Japanese player who has fascinated me since the moment I began to play volleyball. His athleticism and his hardworking mentality have made his presence known on the volleyball court through his heavy spikes and serves. As of now, Yuki Ishikawa is the captain of the Japanese Volleyball team and he is the pillar of support for his teammates.

This webpage was made to introduce players to Volleyball and share my love for it.
